Do you still have questions about sleep? Here are some answers to your frequently asked questions

Question: Do daytime naps affect my sleep?

Answer: If you are constantly taking naps, you could be sleep-deprived. But short naps can be a powerful way to improve performance and lessen fatigue, although long naps could interfere with sleeping at "regular" times and will break your cicadian sleep rhythm.
