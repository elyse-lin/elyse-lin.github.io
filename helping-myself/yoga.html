Yoga Tips

Yoga has many benefits for humans including:

  • physical exercise
  • healing benefits
  • practicing mindfulness and being present
  • stress relief
  • flexibility

Types of Yoga

There are many types and lineages of yoga including vinyasa, hatha, yin, ashtanga, and more. You can do yoga in your home by signing up for a virtual class or looking for free yoga videos on youtube. Yoga is a good skill to learn to keep your mind calm in stressful and turbulent times. Read more on the benefits of yoga here.
