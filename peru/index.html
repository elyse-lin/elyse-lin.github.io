Our Mission
To enable children to receive an online education through cellphones



How Can You Help?
We need to collect cellphones to distribute to children in remote areas so that they can contact their teachers through the internet. Cell phones are especially needed now while we're in a pandemic. The kids don't need a fancy phone, just one that can text and call.
