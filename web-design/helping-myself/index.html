10 Tips To De-Stress

  1. Do yoga
  2. Drink water
  3. Eat nutritious food
  4. Get some sleep
  5. Look at pictures that make you happy
  6. Practice meditation
  7. Develop a physical self-care routine (ie. take a bath, put on a face mask, etc.)
  8. Talk to some friends
  9. Hangout with a pet
  10. Spend time outside

Things Elyse's Does To De-Stress

    Cooking & Baking: My favorite thing to make are cookies

    Work out: I like to go in the pool for a swim or on a Peloton bike

    Shopping: In-person shopping and online shopping. I have a retail shopping addiction

    Organize: I rearrange the items in my room, like my books or the clothes in my closet

    Listen to music: Preferably sad music
