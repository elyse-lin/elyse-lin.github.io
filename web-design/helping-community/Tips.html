Here are 5 tips to fall a sleep

Tip #1: Don't Eat a Big Meal Before Bed

Eat at least a few hours before you head to bed to give your body time to digest the meal thoroughly

Tip #2: Do Some Breathing Exercises

Focusing on breathing slowly and rhythmically will calm your mind and slow your body right down ready full a night of deep slumber

Tip #3: Read a Book Before Bed

Reading lets your brain switch off from the everyday stresses of modern life. It makes your mind, eyes, and body have a rest from all the electronic devices and screens that are everywhere.

Tip #4: Wear Socks

When your body is about to sleep, the blood that flows to your feet and hands is redirected to other core areas to redistribute heat. Wearing compression socks to bed will warm your feet and allow this to happen.

Tip #5: Keep your Room Cool

An excellent hack to fall asleep faster is to keep your bedroom at a cool temperature — but not too cold. Body temperature naturally drops when you begin to fall asleep. By maintaining this cooler temperature in the environment, you will increase your chance of a good night’s sleep.
